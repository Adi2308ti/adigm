Rice Pudding

Rice pudding

Here's a quick and simple rice pudding recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup white rice (preferably short-grain or arborio)
  • 2 cups milk (whole milk works best)
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon (optional)
  • Pinch of salt

    • Instructions:


  • Cook the Rice:In a medium saucepan, combine the rice, 1 1/2 cups of milk, and a pinch of salt. Bring to a boil over medium heat, then reduce the heat to low. Simmer for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the rice is soft and most of the milk has absorbed.
  • Add Sugar and Remaining Milk: Stir in the sugar, vanilla, and the remaining 1/2 cup of milk. Cook for another 5-10 minutes, stirring frequently, until the pudding thickens to your desired consistency.
  • Serve: Once thickened, remove from heat and let it cool slightly. Sprinkle with cinnamon if desired, and serve warm or chilled.

    • Enjoy your quick and creamy rice pudding!
